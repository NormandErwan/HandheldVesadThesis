    \subsubsection{3.1. Task completion time}\label{task-completion-time}

    We perform a full factorial ANOVA with the model: TCT \textasciitilde{}
TECHNIQUE x TEXT\_SIZE x DISTANCE + TECHNIQUE x ORDERING.

We should test all the assumptions of an ANOVA : independence of measure
points, normality, homogeneity of variance. The trials are independent
from each other, since they are generated randomly. The data is
non-normal, but the ANOVA can tolerate non-normal data with skewed
distribution. The homogeneity of variance is less important when the
sample sizes are equal.
